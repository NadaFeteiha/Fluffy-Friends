Pet Care Guide

Category Tips
Nutrition - Provide age-appropriate food (puppy, adult, senior).
- Follow feeding guidelines based on weight and activity level.
- Ensure access to fresh, clean water at all times.
Exercise - Engage in daily physical activity suited to the pet (e.g., walks, playtime).
- Use interactive toys to stimulate mental and physical health.
- Avoid over-exertion, especially in hot or cold weather.
Grooming - Brush fur regularly to reduce shedding and prevent matting.
- Bathe as needed based on breed and lifestyle.
- Trim nails every 3â€“4 weeks to avoid overgrowth and discomfort.
Health Care - Schedule regular vet check-ups and vaccinations.
- Use flea, tick, and worm prevention as recommended.
- Watch for signs of illness, like appetite loss, lethargy, or unusual behavior.
Training - Teach basic commands like sit, stay, and come.
- Use positive reinforcement techniques (treats and praise).
- Be patient and consistent with house training and behavioral corrections.
Environment - Provide a safe, clean, and comfortable living space.
- Remove hazards like toxic plants, cords, or small objects they might swallow.
- Use pet gates or crates for safety when unsupervised.
Social Interaction - Spend quality time bonding with your pet daily.
- Socialize them with other animals and people from an early age.
- Monitor interactions with children to ensure mutual safety.
